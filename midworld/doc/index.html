Introduction

Set in a post-apocalyptic world much like our own, Midworld challenges players to keep their wits while fending off the evil hordes as they search for the reason they exist. Or something like that. Insert cool marketing bites here as the design is fleshed out.

Miworld is an open source game being developed by the ISU Game Developers Club. It is licensed under the LGPL and can be used freely. It runs on Windows, Linux and Irix.

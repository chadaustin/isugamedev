A.  Starting point of the level.  Emaciated bodies lying around, start with no weapons.
B.  These are slow turrets.  If you run fast enough, you can get by without taking much damage.
C.  A small ammo box, an empty pistol, and a small medkit.
D.  Some medium-difficulty droids.  One of them drops the shotgun or spreadgun or something.
E.  This gate has to be destroyed.
F.  Lots of droids.  They drop ammo and weapons (assault rifle?).
G.  Fast turrets....  need the assault rifle to kill them.
H.  Two gates that must be destroyed.
I.  The big boss dude.  Lots of hit points.  When it is killed, the gate to the dock opens.
J.  The goal of the level.

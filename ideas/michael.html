[1] Introduction

     Michael is a computer game based in today’s time period.  It can be classified as a modified Role Playing Game.  The game is played as a single player game.  Michael is the main character, whom happens to be of an unknown race.  He has special powers that most people do not.  He can be considered a sorcerer, if only he knew.

 

[2] Game Design

    

     [2.1] View and Game Play

The game will have a third person top-down view point.  The game will be similar to that of Oni for those who have played that.  The player’s view is directly behind the character and to the top.  There will be a slight spring to the camera as well, meaning that the camera will follow after a slight time delay.

 

[2.1.1] Head Turning

The player will have the ability to turn his head from side to side without having his whole body move.  This will in turn rotate the camera around his body giving the player the ability to see left and right and around corners and such.

 

[2.1.2] Movement

Moving the character, Michael, will involve both mouse and keyboard use.  The mouse will control actions being performed along with rotation and turning of the on-screen character.  The keyboard will control things like going forward, backward, strafing left and right, etc.  This will also be customizable by the user playing the game.

 

[3] Characters/Players

 

[3.1] Michael

He is the main character in the story.  Michael is a young man when the story begins, still living at home with his parents, say around the age of 19.

 

[3.2] Xamen, Michael’s Father

This is Michael’s father.  He is killed in the opening of the game and his journal is found which begins Michael on his new journey.

 

[4] Story Line

 

This is basically the opening sequence of the game, the intro movie so to speak.  Here the player recognizes himself with the brief history up to now and begins his journey through Michael.

 

[4.1] Michael’s Father

Since the beginning of time there has existed a secret society.  This unknown race has lived among us since the beginning of time, without our knowledge.  It is a secret society of Mages, or magic users.  They have the ability to control and manipulate certain arcane energies with only the thought of their minds.  The society has been successful in hiding this from the rest of us.  They are among us and practice their magic among us, but we don’t see them in any other way than a normal human being.  The gift is hereditary and it is almost unheard of for a mage to wed outside of the society.

Michael’s father was and still is one of the most powerful of all of this society.  His powers are a mystery because he, himself, is not a full blood.  In other words his father was a mage, but his mother was not.  His father has faced and lived a very difficult life, full of peril and hard decisions.  Because of certain circumstances Michael’s father decided to flee and not live his life as a mage any longer.  He used all of his abilities and powers to find a way to hide himself from everyone.  He succeeded, and his best friend joined him.  That was years ago and today Michael’s father lives a normal life, free from his arcane abilities.  He has raised a family and has one son…..Michael.  He has never spoke of his past, or his lineage to anyone since he left.

A death has just occurred in Michael’s family.  Michael’s father has mysteriously been slain while he was walking through the woods near his house.  There are no marks what-so-ever on the body, no poison detected, nothing wrong with his internal organs.  It is as if his soul was taken and his body fell lifeless behind.

 

[4.2] Michael’s Find

The death of Michael’s father has cut deeply into Michael’s being.  He feels lost and alone in the world right now.  Always knowing that for some reason he has been a little different than everyone else he has known, he know doesn’t have anyone to turn to.  Michael has no idea of his lineage or that he does in fact have this ability of sorcery.

Weeks have passes since his father’s death and Michael has been fighting an overwhelming urge to go to where his father was found dead.  He can no longer resist and finally gives in to this strange feeling.  When Michael arrives at the spot he instantly knows, or better, feels with all of his being, that his father was murdered.  A flash of light strikes him, along with a short frame of his father’s death.  Again it hits him, more vivid this time, showing him more and more of how his father was murdered.  Michael is overwhelmed by these visions and drops to the ground falling unconscious.

When Michael awakes he sees a strange rock under the brush in front of him.  Lying on the ground he reaches out for it and just before his hand is about to tough it, the rock begins to glow brightly from within and it seems as if the light is carving some strange symbol onto the stone.  Michael feels surprisingly at ease, as if it’s something he’s seen for years now.  He reaches out for it again and as he touches it the rock once again comes to life.  He feels ideas and feelings and thoughts rushing into his brain from an unknown source.

Michael feels a little different after having touched the unique stone.  He feels someone uneasy, yet, for some strange reason, he feels a little bit stronger.

 

[4.3] The Journal

For some unknown Michael has lost the whereabouts of the stone.  It has disappeared, apparently.  Confused and wondering as to if he has just imagined the whole thing, Michael goes home.

When Michael returns home he goes into his father’s old study in the basement and begins looking around, searching for something.  He is not sure what he is looking for, perhaps some kind of closure to his father’s death, some wanting to know what he saw in the forest, something, anything.

Just as Michael was giving up in frustration, he caught a glint from something on the floor in the Study’s small closet.  Michael bends down for a closer inspection and the object came into full few.  It seems to be the same kind of symbol on the mysterious rock that he thought he hallucinated about in the woods.  It’s a carving into the basement floor, it seems as if the cement was melted away, not cut out by some type of chisel.

Michael bends down to feel the symbol as he did earlier to the rock and the instant he touches it the symbol glows blindingly bright.  The ground slices open in a square surrounding the symbol as it fades away, sinking underground itself.  The square around where the symbol used to be starts to rise and brings the small square of floor with it.  Michael is in complete awe and seems to be frozen with what he is witnessing.  The floor comes all the way off the ground and disintegrates into nothing.  The light instantly begins to fade away to nothing as it were before whatever happened just occurred.

Michael, now breathing heavier and heavier slowly walks forward to the edge of the floor where the square rose up and vanished.  He peers in to find a very unexpected item.  A book.  Perplexed by everything that has just happened, Michael reaches in and lifts the book out of the hole.

There is a seal surrounding the book, it cannot be opened.  Michael also notices that there are some strange writings on its cover, but doesn’t pay close attention to them at first.  He tries to force the book open with a letter opener on his father’s desk, but to no avail, for this book has been locked by magical means.  Michael takes a closer look at the writings on the cover and for some strange reason, he believes he can understand what is said.  Michael begins to read aloud, not quite sure of what he is saying, but knowing for sure that this in not English.  In a flash of pure blue the binding vanishes and the book opens.  There is an inscription in the front cover.  Again, it is written in whatever language this is.  Michael can read it.  The inscription says, “Xamen”.  Michael will find out later that this is his father’s given name.

 

[4.4] Following the Journal

This is the story up till now.  After finding what will soon turn out to be his father’s journal, Michael begins his own quest, at first to figure out what his newly discovered skill is all about, and why his father was murdered.  Then Michael will have to make a decision which will effect the outcome of the rest of mankind as we know it.

 

[5] Unique Features

     There are to be many things associated with this game that no other game has either attempted to try or failed when they did try.

 

[5.1] Modifiable Terrain

The world Michael lives in is not unlike the world we live in.  Outside forces can effect and mold the world around us.  For example, a fireball being thrown into the local quick trip would pretty much destroy of ton of stuff.  This kind of real-to-life modification will be used in game as well.

 

[5.2] Guns Mixed With Magic

Just as in real life today there are firearms that people carry around and use, there will be firearms in Michael as well.  Police for instance will use guns, but most mage’s won’t.  They are strictly magic users.

 

[6] Target Platform

The target platform for Michael is to consist of Windows and Linux based computers.

 

[7] Target Audience

Pretty much anyone who loves really involved deep games in which action and thinking are uniquely swirled together to create one hell of a game.

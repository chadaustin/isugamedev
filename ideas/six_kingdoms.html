Backstory

Zoldur is dead. Of this you are certain. In Mage's Council you argued against his plan to perform the Great Alignment ritual. The issue split the Six Kingdoms into two opposing factions. The Dark Ones, Mages of the Ogres, Kobolds, and Hobgoblins, supported Zoldur's plan and now seek to capture K'ulwak province for another attempt at the ritual. Lacking Zoldur's powers, the Dark Ones are even less likely to succeed than their former master. Already the effects of one botched ritual plague the Six Kingdoms. The Dark Ones must not be allowed a second attempt.

Recognition of your wisdom in opposing the plan has raised you to head an alliance of Humans, Elves, and Dwarves wishing to heal the troubled land. Marshal your resources, choose well your kings and commanders. Train your armies and mages to assist them. Expand your influence and power for the battle may last many human lifetimes. But act - someone must rule the unruly races of the Six Kingdoms. Zoldur is dead. Of this you are certain.
